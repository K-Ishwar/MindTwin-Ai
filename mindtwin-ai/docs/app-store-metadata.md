## Full Description (4000 chars max — used for both App Store and Google Play)

MindTwin AI is your personal AI study companion that actually understands how you learn.

Unlike generic study apps, MindTwin creates a **Digital Twin** of you — a learning profile that evolves with every quiz you take, every session you complete, and every mood you log. It spots your weak topics before exam day, predicts when you're getting stressed, and gives you a study plan that fits your life.

**KEY FEATURES:**

🧠 **Personal Digital Twin**
Your AI profile learns your pace, patterns, and knowledge gaps. The more you use MindTwin, the smarter it gets about what you need.

📅 **Smart Study Planner**
Adaptive schedule that updates as you progress. Missed a session? MindTwin adjusts automatically — no guilt, just a new plan.

🔍 **Gap Detection**
Pinpoints exactly which concepts need revision using advanced adaptive quizzes powered by Item Response Theory (IRT). No more guessing what to study.

😌 **Stress Predictor**
Warns you when you're heading toward burnout — before it happens. Log your daily mood and let MindTwin's LSTM model track your wellbeing over time.

🏆 **Earn Your Screen Time**
Complete study tasks to unlock social media time. Gamified focus system that makes studying feel rewarding, not punishing.

📊 **Progress Insights**
See exactly how your ability in each topic grows over time. Visual charts show your improvement week by week.
